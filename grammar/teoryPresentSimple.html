Present Simple

We use the present simple for things that are true in general, or for things that happen sometimes or all the time.

1. Positive

Pronouns Present simple verb
I work, watch, do, etc.
You
We
They
He works, watches, does, etc.
She
It

Spelling

a. When the verb end in s/sh/ch and the pronouns are he, she and it, add es.
  • Pass → Passes
  • Finish → Finishes
  • Watch → Watches
b. When the verb end in y and the pronouns are he, she and it, change it for ies.
  • Study → Studies
  • Try → Tries

Examples

  • I like Math.
  • The university opens at 8 o'clock.
  • Alex studies very hard.
  • The Earth goes around the Sun.
  • We do a lot of thing in our free time.
  • This laptop costs much money
  • The children play video games every evining.
  • She loves the music.
  • They make dinner.
  • There rains every day.
