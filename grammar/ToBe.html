Verb To Be

1. Affirmative

Pronouns Present simple Short form
I am I'm
He is He's
She She's
It It's
You are You're
We We're
They They're

Examples

  • I'm tired
  • My brother (he) is very tall.
  • She's so cute.
  • The pen (it) is green.
  • You're bored.
  • We're peruvians.
  • Mayra and Kevin (they) are friends.

2. Negative

Pronouns Present simple Short form
I am not I'm not
He is not He's not or He isn't
She She's not or She isn't
It It's not or It isn't
You are not You're not or You aren't
We We're not or We aren't
They They're not or They aren't

Examples

  • I'm not hungry.
  • Kevin (he) isn't upset.
  • Mayra (she) isn't lazy.
  • it isn't a dog.
  • You aren't tired.
  • We aren't from US.
  • They aren't roommates.
